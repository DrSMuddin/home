Molecular Biologist and Protein Engineer with the expertise of protein expression, purification and characterization, cell culture and molecular biological techniques for the discovery of antibody based next generation cancer therapeutics. Expertise on cDNA cloning using both the traditional primer and duplexed DNA methods, sequencing, qPCR, rtPCR and western blots to investigate the effectiveness of the nanoformulated therapeutics on cancer cells and mouse models. He is serving Daffodil International University as associate professor from October 2018. He achieved his B Sc and M S degree in Chemistry from the Department of Chemistry of University of Dhaka, Bangladesh. His undergraduate research was performed at Material Chemistry Research Laboratory of University of Dhaka. He achieved the Doctor of Life Science degree from the Laboratory of Biomolecular Adaptation Science of Graduate School of Life Science, Hokkaido University, Sapporo, Japan with the financial aid of Japanese Government (Monbukagakusho: MEXT). His devotion to researches and the scholastic supervision of Professor Sakae Tsuda offered him the opportunity to publish high impacted journals with unique findings. Which help him to be selected as AIST-RA and pursue his postdoctoral researches in the Department of Biological engineering in TUFTS University, MA, USA and Neurology Research Division of Henry Ford Hospital, Detroit, USA. His postdoctoral studies were focused on introducing non-canonical amino acids to prepare specific inhibitors for metzincin and nano-formulation of drugs for the in vivo and in vitro studies on mouse models of Glioma.
